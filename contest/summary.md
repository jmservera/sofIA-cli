# sofIA: The AI Discovery & Delivery Agent

**sofIA** is an agentic CLI tool that transforms the AI Discovery Workshop from a theoretical brainstorming session into a tangible delivery engine. Built on the **GitHub Copilot SDK**, it guides facilitators and teams through a structured 12-step process—from analyzing business needs to generating functional code.

## The Problem

Organizations struggle to bridge the gap between _identifying_ AI opportunities and _validating_ them. conceptual workshops often end with sticky notes and vague requirements, leading to momentum loss, "blank page" paralysis, and a lack of alignment between business goals and technical execution.

## The Solution

sofIA reimagines the facilitator's role by acting as an intelligent agent that doesn't just chat—it does the work. It facilitates the entire lifecycle of AI adoption in a single session.

## Key Capabilities

- **Context-Aware Discovery (WorkIQ):** Connects securely to Microsoft 365 data (emails, meetings, documents) to ground the workshop in actual business reality rather than assumptions.
- **External Intelligence (Microsoft Foundry):** Leveraging **Microsoft Foundry** for robust web search and external knowledge retrieval, ensuring the discovery process is informed by the latest market trends and technological capabilities.
- **Structured Ideation:** Digitizes the "AI Envisioning Cards" methodology to systematically brainstorm, score, and map use cases to business workflows.
- **Autonomous Decision Making:** Automatically evaluates generated ideas against a value/feasibility matrix to _select_ the most promising candidate for implementation.
- **Iterative Delivery (The Ralph Loop):** A specialized coding agent loop that autonomously plans, writes, tests, and fixes code to generate a working Proof of Concept (PoC) repository.

## Impact

sofIA delivers a complete artifacts package—including detailed analysis documents, architectural plans, and a running code project—reducing time-to-prototype by estimated 300% and ensuring every PoC is rooted in verified business value and Responsible AI best practices.
